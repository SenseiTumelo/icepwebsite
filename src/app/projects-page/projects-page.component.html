The Mentorship system would provide students, and mentors the necessary platform to create and manage appointments. It would as such, provide a comprehensive structure of data storage. This enabling the superuser (facilitator) to:

  1. Actively track users’ active use of the system.
  2. Monitor the impact mentorship has had on students’ academic performances.
  3. Provide a tools and functionalities required to demonstrate and report issues, or pain points that exist within modules.

Van tuka lounge was formed in 2013, launched on the 26th of December. The correct spelling of the afrikaans words is “Van Toeka”, meaning “Way Back”. The lounge was inspired by old skool music, they wanted to target older and mature generation. They used sophisticated designs and high quality furniture and finishes of which it’s theme is “Modern meets Vintage”

Artphin Consulting (Pty) Limited was established in 2012 as a private company with the sole purpose of entering the Short-term Insurance industry-with particular emphasis on the Taxi Industry. At the time of the formal registration of the company, the founder, Nanka Phineas Desando was employed as Head of the Short-term and Medical Aid unit (Inland) for Atfin Consulting, a company with offices in Western Cape.
